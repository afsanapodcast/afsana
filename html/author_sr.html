Sandhya Rathore

Sandhya Rathore is an engineer, mother, daughter, sister, friend, and — in the midst of being all of these — a writer. She likes to dream; some of these have come true. She's been interested in writing since childhood. Over time, she's seen many faces of life — some good, some bad — and when she found herself unable to speak out, she wrote. With gusto. Her heart is that of a poet's — like glass. It's endured several cracks but she treasures it nevertheless. Her writings are but descriptions of events in life around her; it is her trifling attempt to string them through words.
