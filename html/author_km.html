Kumudavalli Arun Murthy

Kumudavalli is a voice-over artiste who's worked with AIR Bangalore, Vividh Bharati, and FM Rainbow 101.3. She was with Zee Kannada for seven years as a promo script writer and channel voice. Kumudavalli is now working as a Kannada voice artiste for Airtel, Aircel, Vodafone, Tata Docomo, and Idea. She also writes scripts for the Udaya Kannada channel. She has appeared on stage, and in TV serials and Kannada cinema. She tweets from @kumudarun1.
